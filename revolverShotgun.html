Shotgun moon clips for CO2 revolver

The WinGun line of CO2 revolvers have to high muzzle velocity for Airsoft games, these moon clips fire six BBs at once whoch reduces muzzle velocity and the individual BB power.
The moon clip contains six shells as a unit to speed reloading, very important when you only get six shots before needing to reload.
The 3D printing filrs are published on Thingiverse
